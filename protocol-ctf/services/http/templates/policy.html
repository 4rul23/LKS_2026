Crew Protocols

Mandatory guidelines for all Astral Express crew members.

01

Stellaron Security

Do not share synchronization keys with anyone. This includes Sampo, the Masked Fools, and anyone from IPC "Special Acquisitions".

02

Terminal Security

Lock your terminal before leaving the Parlor Car. Pom-Pom monitors unsecured terminals. Current fine: 500 credits.

03

Anomaly Reporting

Report unauthorized IPC broadcasts or Stellaron anomalies to the Navigator immediately. Do not investigate alone.

04

Fuel Conservation

Reserves are critical. Minimize unnecessary jumps. Coffee runs to Belobog are not emergencies.
